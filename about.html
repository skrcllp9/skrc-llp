Company Introduction

Shri Krishna Road Carrier was established in 1992 by Shri Vijay Kumar Gupta with the vision of providing dependable and efficient transportation services across India. Over the years, the company has grown into a trusted logistics partner for industries and government organizations, known for its commitment to reliability, safety, and timely delivery.
With decades of experience in the transport sector, Shri Krishna Road Carrier continues to serve clients with a strong fleet network, skilled drivers, and a professional operational team dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of service.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and efficient transportation services to our clients. We are committed to meeting the evolving logistics needs of businesses and government institutions. Through strong operational practices, we ensure timely and secure delivery of every consignment. We uphold the highest standards of professionalism in all our interactions and services. Our focus is on delivering consistent quality and achieving complete customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become one of India’s most trusted and preferred logistics partners. We strive to deliver exceptional service quality and dependable logistics solutions. By leveraging innovation and efficient operations, we aim to exceed client expectations. We are dedicated to fostering long-term partnerships built on integrity and trust. Our vision is to create lasting value and drive growth for every client we serve.
