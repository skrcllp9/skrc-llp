Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how Pioneer Healthcare uses cookies on our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as outlined in this policy.​

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They help websites function efficiently and provide useful information to website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use
2.1 Essential Cookies
  • Required for the proper functioning of the website
  • Enable core features like security and accessibility
2.2 Analytical / Performance Cookies
  • Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Allow us to improve website performance and user experience
2.3 Functionality Cookies
  • Remember your preferences and choices
  • Provide a more personalized experience
2.4 Advertising / Targeting Cookies
  • Track browsing behavior to show relevant ads
  • Help deliver marketing content suited to your interests
3. Consent to Use Cookies

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies. If you do not agree, you can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Managing Cookies

4.1 Browser Settings

  • Most browsers allow you to control cookies
  • You can block or delete cookies through browser settings

4.2 Third-Party Tools

  • You can use browser extensions or third-party tools to manage cookies
5. Cookie Duration
  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored on your device until they expire or are deleted
6. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Please review this page periodically for updates.
